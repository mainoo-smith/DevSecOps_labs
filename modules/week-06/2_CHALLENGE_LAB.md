# 🚀 Week 6 Challenge — Production-Ready Container

## 🎯 Goal

Package your microservice as a secure, production-ready container.

---

## 5W1H

- **Who**: Your entire team will deploy this.
- **What**: Multi-stage build, minimal base image.
- **When**: Each release.
- **Where**: Cloud registry → Kubernetes next week.
- **Why**: Trust, speed, security.
- **How**: Scan, sign, push.

---

## Steps

1️⃣ Refactor Dockerfile to multi-stage.
2️⃣ Use a minimal base (e.g., Alpine, Distroless).
3️⃣ Scan and fix critical CVEs.
4️⃣ Sign with Cosign.
5️⃣ Push to Docker Hub or ECR.
6️⃣ Write `week6/container-playbook.md` describing your process.
